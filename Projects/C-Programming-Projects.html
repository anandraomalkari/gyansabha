Mensuration

The field of Mensuration, within mathematics, focuses on the study of geometric figure calculations. This division explores the measurement and properties of various shapes and figures, allowing mathematicians to analyze and understand their characteristics. Mensuration encompasses a wide range of concepts, including the calculation of area, perimeter, volume, surface area, and other related measurements.
